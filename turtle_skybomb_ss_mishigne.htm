I finally decided to turn it public. Its a bit complicated and maybe 1 or 2 factors are a bit imprecise. Feel free to fix (tell me too).

Turtle SS Skybomb by M1sh1gn3

Screen divided in 20 parts (800 pixels).

For null wind



Shooting with wind

First of all, you must know the power and angle for 0 wind. After it, you fix the angle with the angle chart and fix the power with the power chart for the desired wind. (Same as trico)

Angle windchart:



How to fix the angle and power:

New angle = 0 wind angle +/- wind x windfactor (angle chart)
New power = 0 wind power +/- wind x windfactor (power chart)

Example:

Wind 20 poiting up (90 degrees) and target at half screen:

Angle / power for 0 wind: 83.76 / 3.70

Fixing the Angle: 83.76 – 20 x 0.07 = 82.36 ~ 82
Fixing the Power: 3.70 – 20 x 0.035 = 3.00

Wind 14 poiting exactly against you (180 degrees) and target at part 8:

Angle / power for 0 wind: 85.00 / 3.69

Fixing the angle: 85.00 – 14 x 0.52 = 77.72 ~ 78
Fixing the power: 3.69 + 14 x 0.006 = 3.77

Note:

On the Angle Windchart, there are some numbers in red. These numbers are the biggest wind that i recommend you to use this kind of SS (bigger winds require more than 4 bars of power).

If you are backshooting with skybomb, pay attention for the correct angle. Backshots are much sensitive to the angle.
